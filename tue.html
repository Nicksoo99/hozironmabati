About Horizon Mabati
Factory Ltd

Horizon Mabati Factory Limited is a leading manufacturer and supplier of premium roofing solutions in Kenya. From corrugated and box profile sheets to stone-coated tiles, we deliver quality straight from our factory floors to your roof — at prices that protect your budget as well as your home.

With branches in Nairobi, Kisumu, and Eldoret, we serve thousands of homeowners, contractors, and developers across Kenya. Our mission is simple: build trust, one roof at a time.
